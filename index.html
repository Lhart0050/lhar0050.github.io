ABOUT US

Grand Laguna

Founded in 1992 Grand Laguna was refurbished at the end of 2018. Grand Laguna managed by Laguna Group. A company that also manage Pondok Laguna in Carlton, Victoria and OKO OKO Bali, Indonesia.

Grand Laguna stocks a wide range of South East Asian products that originated from Indonesia, Malaysia, Singapore and Thailand along with selected products from Philippine, Vietnam, Japan, Korea, China and Colombia.
